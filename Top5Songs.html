My Top 5 Songs This Month

#5 Let Me Go

Daniel Caesar

This song is about Daniel realizing that he has ran out of patience with his spouse and that their relationship is not going well. he needs his partner's permission to depart because he wants to separate.

#4 Bloody Daisies

Yung Kage, Yumi, Softwilly, Larrycroft

Back in June of this year, Softwilly teased Bloody Daisies as "Japan Song" in his teaser videos. To advertise it, the first official teaser was unveiled on August 9. It is assumed that Softwilly's brief journey to Japan, along with that of many other group chat participants, is what led to the creation of this song. When it came to this song, Softwilly only wanted to create something that sounded like the opening of an anime.

#3 Always

Daniel Caesar

Daniel thinks about his ex-girlfriend; she's moved on, but he wants to continue as they were. His ever-evolving perspective is demonstrated by the discrepancy in viewpoints on his relationships throught the record. Although his reflections demonstrate his development, his ex-lover is constantly reminded that their love wasn't only a sentiment existing within a happy partnership but endures faultlessly even after their split.

#2 New Tooth

Rich Brian

Rich Brian considers his path to success and the sacrifices he had to make in "New Tooth." The song examines issues of self-actualization, fortitude, and the pressure to be successful in the music business. Brian's reflective lyrics offer a window into his thought and the difficulties he encounters as an artist.

#1 drive ME crazy!

Lil Yachty

This song opens the second part of Lil Yachty's album "Lets Start Here." Lil Yachty and Diana Gordon, who sings the entire first stanza, both discuss how their passion for one another drives them insane. One section of the album, this one, is heavily influenced by neo-soul.
